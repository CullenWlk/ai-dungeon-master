# The Senate of Eos
Type: faction
Tags: faction, senate, government, eos

The Senate is the ruling body of the Eos Republic, composed primarily of human representatives from major cities and noble families.

It is responsible for legislation, trade regulation, and military decisions. While officially unified, internal rivalries between city representatives influence many outcomes.

---

# The Coastal Houses
Type: faction
Tags: faction, nobles, trade, eos

The Coastal Houses are wealthy families based primarily in Hadria and Bosona, controlling trade routes, agriculture, and economic policy.

They hold significant influence within the Senate and often compete for dominance over trade and resources.

---

# The Fleet of Sevona
Type: faction
Tags: faction, military, navy, eos

The Fleet of Sevona is the naval force of the republic, responsible for protecting trade routes and projecting power across surrounding waters.

Its leadership is closely tied to the city of Sevona, giving that region significant military influence.

---

# The Wardens of Vedinum
Type: faction
Tags: faction, frontier, vedinum, eos

The Wardens are a loosely organized group responsible for maintaining safety near the mountains and forests surrounding Vedinum.

They deal with threats from wildlife, ruins, and other dangers, operating with more independence than other formal structures.

---

# The Daughters of Thalorin
Type: faction
Tags: faction, silvae-umbra, elves, amazons, forest, isolationist

An all-elven warrior society that inhabits the depths of Silvae Umbra. The Daughters of Thalorin are descendants of the island’s original inhabitants and consider themselves the rightful protectors of the forest and its ruins.

They reject the authority of the Eos Republic and view outsiders—especially humans—as intruders who have no claim to the land. They operate in small, highly mobile warbands and are known for ambush tactics, archery, and intimate knowledge of the terrain.

---

# The Black Net
Type: faction
Tags: faction, criminal, hadria, trade, smuggling

A sprawling smuggling network operating primarily out of Hadria’s docks and river systems. The Black Net controls illegal trade routes, including contraband, restricted artifacts, and occasionally soul stones.

They maintain influence through bribery, intimidation, and careful avoidance of direct confrontation with the Senate.

---

# The Ashen Veil
Type: faction
Tags: faction, cult, vedinum, silvae-umbra, void

A secretive cult devoted to the Void, operating in the shadows near Vedinum and within the outer reaches of Silvae Umbra. They believe that the collapse of order is necessary for true enlightenment.

Their rituals often involve corrupted arcane practices and attempts to manipulate or awaken ancient forces hidden within the ruins.

---

# The Tidebound Brotherhood
Type: faction
Tags: faction, sevona, maritime, guild, sailors

A powerful maritime brotherhood based in Sevona. While not officially criminal, they operate independently of the Senate and control many aspects of shipping and dock labor.

They enforce their own rules within the harbor and are known for protecting their members at any cost.

---

# The Order of the Pure Flame
Type: faction
Tags: faction, religious, aurora, hadria, purification

A devout order dedicated to Aurora, focused on the cleansing of corruption and the preservation of moral order. They operate openly in Hadria but conduct investigations across the island.

They are respected but feared for their uncompromising stance on corruption and dark practices.

---

# The Verdant Circle
Type: faction
Tags: faction, druidic, bosona, sylvana, nature

A loose network of druids and nature-aligned individuals centered around Bosona and the surrounding farmland. They work to maintain balance between agriculture and the natural world.

They are not hostile but will act against those who exploit the land excessively.

---

# The Iron Syndicate
Type: faction
Tags: faction, vedinum, mining, labor, underground

A powerful labor organization controlling mining and resource extraction in Vedinum. While publicly legitimate, the Syndicate exerts influence through intimidation and control of supply lines.

They are deeply embedded in the economic structure of the region.

---

# The Seekers of the Old Path
Type: faction
Tags: faction, scholars, ruins, eos, elven-history

A group of scholars and explorers dedicated to uncovering the truths behind the ancient elven ruins. They operate across the island but are most active near Silvae Umbra.

They are often in conflict with both the Daughters of Thalorin and the Ashen Veil.
