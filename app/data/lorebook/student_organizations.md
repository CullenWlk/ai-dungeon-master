# Hardenforth Student Organizations
Type: category
Tags: academy, students, organizations, clubs, fraternities

Student organizations at the Hardenforth Academy are divided between general clubs and upperclassman societies. Clubs are open, practical, and tied to study or social life, while fraternities and orders are selective, tradition-bound, and often modeled after old institutional societies.

Most fraternities are restricted to upperclassmen and carry influence in social standing, networking, and post-academy opportunities.
