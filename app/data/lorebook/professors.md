# Hardenforth Academy Faculty
Type: category
Tags: academy, faculty, professors, education

The faculty of the Hardenforth Academy of the Arcane is composed of some of the most accomplished scholars, mages, and instructors across Terra. Each professor specializes in a specific discipline, ranging from foundational studies to advanced arcane practice.

The faculty maintain strict standards of conduct and education, with each department operating under both academic tradition and arcane regulation.

# Headmaster Altheryn Vael
Type: npc
Tags: headmaster, administration, arcane, leadership

Headmaster Altheryn Vael is the current overseer of the Hardenforth Academy.

Appearance:
- High elf male
- Tall and slender with pale skin
- Long silver hair tied neatly at the back
- Wears dark formal robes with subtle gold arcane threading

Description:
Altheryn is calm, precise, and deeply controlled. He rarely raises his voice and commands authority through presence alone. His background lies in high-level arcane theory and planar structure, making him particularly focused on maintaining order within the academy.

He is known to be strict but fair, prioritizing the safety of the academy over all else.

# Professor Alfin Turime
Type: npc
Tags: professor, interplanar, darkithia, student-relations

Age: Mid-40s  
Position: Professor of Interplanar Studies & Upperclassman Student Relations  

Race: Darkithian Elf  

Appearance:
- Olive-toned skin
- Dark, shoulder-length hair often worn loose or tied at the nape
- Well-kept short beard
- Wears layered academic robes with subtle foreign patterns and embroidery
- Carries himself with relaxed, approachable posture

Description:
Alfin Turime is a foreign professor from the elven kingdom of Darkithia, bringing with him a distinct cultural perspective uncommon within Orms. He teaches Interplanar Studies, focusing on the structure of the planes, their boundaries, and the risks involved in interacting with them. His lectures are grounded and practical, often drawing from real experience, but he presents even complex material in a way that feels accessible and engaging.

Beyond his academic role, Alfin serves as the primary authority for upperclassman student relations. He handles disputes, mediates conflicts, and ensures that tensions are resolved before they escalate—especially when arcane ability is involved. Students are often sent to him when situations fall outside the control of standard faculty discipline, though many choose to go to him on their own.

Personality:
Alfin is calm, patient, and quietly warm. He listens fully before responding, making students feel heard even when they are clearly in the wrong. While he does not shy away from enforcing consequences, he frames them as part of growth rather than punishment, which makes his authority easier to accept.

Background Influence:
Coming from Darkithia, Alfin brings a more direct and grounded approach to conflict resolution, but tempers it with an understanding of the pressures students face. He places more value on honesty and intent than on status, which often puts him at odds with more traditional, status-driven expectations within Orms.

Student Interaction:
Upperclassmen tend to see Alfin as someone they can actually talk to, not just report to. Many students who would avoid other faculty members are willing to approach him, knowing he will treat them fairly. Even those who try to test boundaries often find it difficult to dislike him, as he remains consistently respectful and understanding.
