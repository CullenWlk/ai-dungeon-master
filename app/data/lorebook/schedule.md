# Player Academic Schedule
Type: schedule
Tags: academy, classes, student, timetable, fifth-year

The player is an incoming 5th year student at the Hardenforth Academy of the Arcane, admitted late after being discovered to have exceptional arcane ability. Their schedule mixes upperclassman coursework with reinforcement of key fundamentals, placing them among established 4th–6th year students who have already been at the academy for years.

Morning classes include Mathematics & Logical Theory with Professor Halwen Marris. Other students in this class are Nyra Velis, Alric Venn Jr., Lira Voss, Marius Kest, Aedra Solenne, and Thalen Marris.

The player also attends History & Rhetoric with Professor Elyra Voss. Other students in this class are Selene Ardent, Kaelen Rith, Liora Vane, Marcellus Vane Jr., Corin Ashwell, and Elyra Voss Jr.

Midday classes include Spellcasting Fundamentals, Upper Division, with Professor Virella Thorne. Other students in this class are Elira Dawnmere, Virella Solis, Darien Cross, Isara Nyx, and Tharos Vale.

The player also attends Interplanar Studies with Professor Alfin Turime. Other students in this class are Mira Solenne Jr., Serin Vol, Kael Draven, Valen Dray, and Darius Korr.

Afternoon classes include Artificing & Enchantment with Professor Brannik Forgehand. Other students in this class are Brannik Stonewell, Brannor Feld, Corvin Ash, Tomas Alvarez, and Korrin Hale. This course covers enchanted item creation, including the use of soul stones and bound souls as the necessary power source for true enchantment.

The player also attends Magical Combat Training with Professor Arctus Vale. Other students in this class are Mateo Alvarez, Garron Steel, Isandro Cortés, Garrick Thorne, and Valen Dray.

Evening and rotational classes include Astronomy & Astral Study with Professor Mira Solenne. Other students in this class are Mira Solenne Jr., Aedra Solenne, Nyssa Vale, and Renna Sol.

The player also attends Arcane Ethics with Professor Elric Dawnmere. Other students in this class are Seraphine Durel, Liora Windell, Darius Korr, and Kaelen Rith.

Because the player is joining as a late incoming 5th year, their placement may cause social tension. The Silks may see them as an outsider, the Casters may question whether their talent matches years of formal training, and the Blades may test whether they can handle upperclassman pressure.
