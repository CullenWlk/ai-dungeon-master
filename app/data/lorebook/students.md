# Mateo Rellis
Type: npc
Tags: student, roommate, academy

Age: 17  
Year: 5th Year  

Race: Human  

Affiliation:
- Dueling Club
- Clique: The Blades (loosely)

Appearance:
- Medium height, athletic build  
- Warm olive-toned skin  
- Dark wavy hair, usually pushed back but never perfectly kept  
- Easy smile, expressive eyes  
- Often in partially loosened uniform or training gear  

Description:
Mateo Rellis is your assigned roommate in the upper dormitories, and unlike most students, he actually makes the room feel lived in. His side is cleaner than you’d expect, though there’s always something out—practice gloves, a half-finished meal, or a book he swears he’s going to read. He tends to come and go throughout the day, balancing training, classes, and time spent in the city when he can.

He talks easily, not in a way that demands attention, but in a way that fills silence naturally. Whether it’s complaining about a professor, recounting a duel, or asking how your day went, Mateo keeps conversation going without it feeling forced. He’s the type to bring extra food back without saying anything about it or offer help before you ask.

Personality:
Mateo is personable, relaxed, and grounded. He takes things seriously when it matters, especially in training, but doesn’t carry that intensity into everyday life. He reads people well and tends to adjust himself to keep things comfortable, though he’s not afraid to call something out if it needs to be said.

Room Dynamic:
Living with Mateo is easy. He respects space but doesn’t let things go completely silent, often keeping a light conversation going when you’re both around. He’s the kind of roommate who makes the place feel less like a dorm and more like somewhere you actually stay.

# Upperclassman Dorm Room 3B-17
Type: location
Tags: academy, dorms, student-life, interior

Location: Upperclassman Dormitories, Hardenforth Academy  

Occupants:
- The Player  
- Mateo Rellis  

Description:
Dorm Room 3B-17 is a shared living space assigned to the player and Mateo Rellis. It is designed for two upperclassman students, structured for both study and rest. The room is rectangular in layout, with the entrance opening into a central space that naturally divides into two personal halves without a physical barrier.

Layout:
- Two single beds positioned along opposite walls, each with a small bedside table  
- Two desks placed near the windows, typically claimed and personalized by each occupant  
- A shared wardrobe split internally for personal belongings  
- A central open space between beds and desks used for movement, conversation, or occasional sparring practice  

Windows:
- Tall, narrow windows set into the outer wall  
- Provide consistent natural light during the day  
- Overlook the inner campus grounds, including a partial view of the training fields  

Interior Details:
- Walls are plain stone, marked with light wear from previous occupants  
- The player’s side reflects their own habits, while Mateo’s side tends to be lived-in but maintained, often with gear or personal items left out  
- Desks are typically cluttered with coursework, arcane materials, and personal effects  

Lighting:
- Mounted arcane lamps fixed to the walls  
- Provide steady, soft light suitable for late-night study or conversation  

Atmosphere:
The room balances quiet and activity depending on the time of day. When empty, it feels still and functional, but when both occupants are present, it tends to fill with low conversation, study noise, or the occasional sound of Mateo returning from training. It feels less rigid than most dorm spaces due to Mateo’s presence, making it more comfortable over time.

Use:
Dorm Room 3B-17 serves as the shared base of operations for the player and Mateo during the academic year. It is where they study, rest, and interact on a daily basis, gradually taking on a more personal and familiar character as the year progresses.
