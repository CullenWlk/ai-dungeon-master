# The Planes of Coia
Type: category
Tags: planes, cosmology, existence, arcana

The Planes of Coia are the layered dimensions that make up reality, each containing its own three-dimensional space where life, matter, and celestial bodies may exist.

All planes are contained within the Outer Plane, which stabilizes them against the infinite and chaotic forces of raw arcana beyond. Between planes lies astral space, a shifting expanse of diluted arcane energy.

---

# The Outer Plane
Type: plane
Tags: outer plane, containment, arcana, cosmos

The Outer Plane is the vast boundary that contains all other planes within Coia. It acts as a protective layer, preventing the infinite potential of raw arcana from tearing apart the structured planes inside.

Within it exist countless planes separated by astral space, forming the full structure of the realm.

---

# Astral
Type: concept
Tags: astral, arcana, space, between-planes

The Astral refers to the chaotic, arcane-filled space between planes and beyond the structured realm.

Anything described as Astral exists outside or between planes, within a medium of raw and unstable magical potential.

---

# Major Planes
Type: category
Tags: major planes, cosmology, structure, baseline

The Major Planes are the five primary layers of existence in Coia. They serve as the foundation for measuring and organizing all other planes.

Each holds significant importance to the structure of the realm, including the homes of the gods and the central world of mortals.

---

# Order of the Planes
Type: concept
Tags: cosmology, hierarchy, planes, structure

The planes of Coia are arranged in a vertical structure from highest to lowest:

Celestial → Fey → Material → Underbrim → Void

Spatial size decreases exponentially the further a plane exists from the Material Plane, which is effectively infinite in scale.

---

# Celestial Plane
Type: plane
Tags: celestial, gods, divine, light

The Celestial Plane is the highest plane of Coia and the home of the Celestial House. It represents perfection, order, and divine righteousness.

Its spatial size is relatively small compared to the Material Plane but holds immense divine significance.

---

# Fey Plane
Type: plane
Tags: fey, magic, nature, threshold

The Fey Plane is a major plane positioned between the Celestial and Material planes. It acts as a transitional space and contains pathways to higher divine realms.

Its size is comparable to Terra and is rich with magical and natural energies.

---

# Material Plane
Type: plane
Tags: material, reality, mortals, infinite

The Material Plane is the central and largest plane in Coia, containing the physical universe where mortals live.

Its spatial size is effectively infinite from a mortal perspective and serves as the baseline for all other planes.

---

# Underbrim
Type: plane
Tags: underbrim, hell, dark, lower planes

The Underbrim is a major plane located between the Material Plane and the Void. It serves as a gateway to darker realms and contains the hells of Coia, where damned souls and infernal forces reside.

It is a place where suffering, punishment, and transformation occur, forming the foundation of many dark powers within the realm. It also contains pathways leading deeper into the Void.

Its size is comparable to Terra and is associated with hostile and oppressive environments.

---

# Void
Type: plane
Tags: void, darkness, corruption, abyss

The Void is the lowest plane of Coia and the domain of the Void House. It represents corruption, destruction, and the absence of order.

Like the Celestial Plane, it is relatively small in spatial size but immense in influence.

---

# Minor Planes
Type: category
Tags: minor planes, interstitial, dimensions, cosmology

Minor Planes, also known as Interstitial Planes, are smaller planes that exist between the Major Planes.

They vary in size and nature depending on their proximity to larger planes and often serve as specialized or thematic realms.

---

# Mythic Planes
Type: category
Tags: mythic planes, fey, material, magic

The Mythic Planes are minor planes that exist between the Fey and Material planes. These realms are often shaped by magic, dreams, and natural forces.

They tend to reflect aspects of life, imagination, and myth.

---

# Penumbral Planes
Type: category
Tags: penumbral planes, underbrim, shadow, darkness

The Penumbral Planes are minor planes that exist between the Material Plane and the Underbrim.

These realms are often darker reflections of reality, embodying shadow, decay, and chaotic forces.

---

# The Farshores

# The Verdant

# The Dreamscape

# The Shadowfell
